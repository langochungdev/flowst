Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2026

1. Introduction

At Flowst, we believe that your focus and productivity data belong exclusively to you. This Privacy Policy explains how Flowst operates as a privacy-first, local application and how your data is handled.

2. Data Collection & Storage

Flowst is a fully offline, local-first desktop application. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

  • Local Storage: All data generated by the app—including your Pomodoro focus history, category tags, goals, and customized settings—are stored entirely locally on your machine.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or log in to use Flowst.

3. Telemetry & Analytics

We respect your device and your privacy. Flowst does not include any tracking, telemetry, crash reporting, or analytics software. Your usage patterns are yours alone.

4. Third-Party Services

Flowst does not integrate with any third-party tracking, advertising, or data collection services. The application runs independently on your operating system without making background network requests to third parties.

5. Your Rights and Control

Since all data remains exclusively on your computer, you have absolute control over it at all times. You can modify, export, or permanently delete your local application data files through your operating system's file manager.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our software or legal requirements. Since we do not collect your email address, we cannot notify you directly of these changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this privacy policy or how Flowst handles data, please feel free to reach out to us at langochungdev@gmail.com.
