Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

1. Data Collection

Flowst is a fully offline, local-first application. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. All data generated by the app, including your focus history, categories, and settings, are stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

Flowst does not integrate with third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

3. Your Data

Since all data remains on your machine, you have full control over it. You can export or delete your local files at any time.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at langochungdev@gmail.com.
